Read a document without sending it anywhere.

Faraday runs a language model inside your browser. Drop in a PDF or a photo of a form — it extracts the key fields, summarizes it, and flags anything worth a second look. No upload. No server. No account. Turn off your Wi-Fi and watch it keep working.

Try it in 4 steps:
  1. Wait for the model to finish downloading ("Model ready").
  2. While still online, grab a sample document using the links in the "Your document" box below — or use your own file.
  3. Now turn off your Wi-Fi. Watch the corner flip to "offline · air-gapped."
  4. Drop the document in and analyze. It works with zero bytes sent — even fully disconnected.
It runs slowly because it's on your machine, not a cloud GPU — that's the privacy tradeoff.
